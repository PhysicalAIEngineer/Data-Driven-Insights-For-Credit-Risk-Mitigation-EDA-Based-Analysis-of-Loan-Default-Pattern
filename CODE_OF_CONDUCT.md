# 📜 Code of Conduct

## 🌍 Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a **harassment-free experience for everyone**, regardless of:

* Age
* Body size
* Disability
* Ethnicity
* Gender identity and expression
* Level of experience
* Nationality
* Personal appearance
* Race
* Religion
* Sexual identity and orientation

We are committed to fostering an **open, inclusive, and respectful environment**.

---

## 🤝 Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

* ✔️ Using welcoming and inclusive language
* ✔️ Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
* ✔️ Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
* ✔️ Showing empathy towards other contributors
* ✔️ Being collaborative and supportive

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* ❌ Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments
* ❌ Personal or political attacks
* ❌ Trolling, insulting, or derogatory remarks
* ❌ Publishing others’ private information without permission
* ❌ Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## ⚖️ Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

* Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
* Taking appropriate corrective action in response to violations
* Ensuring a safe and welcoming environment

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

---

## 🚨 Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

* 📩 Report it by contacting: mailto: chetansonigara01@gmail.com
* 📝 Provide details (if possible) such as:

  * Description of the incident
  * Location (issue, PR, discussion, etc.)
  * Any relevant evidence

All reports will be handled **confidentially and fairly**.

---

## 🔒 Enforcement Actions

Violations may result in:

* ⚠️ Warning
* 🚫 Temporary ban
* ❌ Permanent ban from the project

Severity will depend on the nature and frequency of the violation.

---

## 📌 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

* Project repository (issues, PRs, comments)
* Community interactions related to this project
* Official communication channels
